## Phase 16B-3 Coverage Notes

The same seeded buyer/brand/admin records support the new non-manual review management surfaces:

| Surface | Seed support |
| --- | --- |
| Buyer My Reviews | Editable, expired, deleted, approved, hidden, flagged, and pending moderation buyer-owned review states are seeded. The default `/reviews/me` path excludes deleted reviews unless `includeDeleted=true`. |
| Brand Reviews Dashboard | Brand-owned approved, hidden, flagged, pending moderation, and product review states are seeded for lifecycle dashboard filters and summary counts. |
| Brand report/flag | The seeded brand owns the seeded review targets, so report/flag can be tested against `POST /brands/reviews/lifecycle/:reviewId/report`. |
| Admin Review Analytics | Seeded lifecycle statuses, satisfaction values, brand IDs, and product IDs support total/status/target/satisfaction/top-brand/top-product analytics. |
